Questions Answered

Can I compete solo or do I need a team? ▼
Both! Solo entrants and teams are judged separately and compete for their own medals. No competition between the two formats—equal prestige, separate podiums.
What's the difference between $25 and $30? ▼
$25 is early bird pricing (until Sept 30). After Sept 30, pricing goes to $30 per student. That's it—no hidden fees.
Where are finals and what's included? ▼
Finals are December 4–6 at Cyberport, Hong Kong (or Bangkok as backup). Registration includes online qualifying exam access, live presentation slot, judge feedback, and certificate. Travel/accommodation are on you.
Do you offer group or school discounts? ▼
Yes. 10+ students from the same school = $20/student early bird (save $5 per person). Email competitions@emeraldhv.com for bulk quotes.
What if I don't make finals? ▼
All qualifiers get detailed feedback from judges. You'll know exactly what worked and what to improve. Many students return Year 2 significantly stronger.
Do you offer financial aid or scholarships? ▼
Contact us at competitions@emeraldhv.com to discuss scholarship opportunities and payment plans. We work with schools and organizations on group sponsorships.
