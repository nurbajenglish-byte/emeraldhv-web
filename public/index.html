Who We Are

An Education Group Built for the Asian Century

Emerald Horizon Ventures is a Hong Kong-registered education company operating across Southeast and East Asia. We build B2B programs and platforms that help schools deliver better outcomes — and students access better opportunities.

Our work spans international study tours, student competitions, school recruitment, and education consulting — united by a single belief: great education changes lives.

We work exclusively business-to-business, partnering with language centers, bilingual schools, and international institutions as our distribution and delivery partners.

🌏
Asia-First Strategy
Built around direct-flight corridors and regional school holiday calendars. Vietnam, China, Myanmar, and Thailand are our core operating markets.
🤝
Pure B2B Model
We sell turnkey programs to schools and centers — not direct to parents. Our partners earn margin; we handle operations.
🎓
Outcomes-Driven
Every program produces a tangible credential — a certificate, a competition result, a school placement — not just an experience.
