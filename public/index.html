The Founder

Built by Someone
Who's Been Inside Schools

Emerald Horizon Ventures was founded by Nurbakyt Boltayev — an international education consultant, Cambridge & CIS specialist, and author with hands-on experience transforming schools across Asia.

Every venture under the EHV umbrella was built from a simple belief: education is the most powerful force for changing a life's trajectory — and the institutions that deliver it deserve better tools, better programs, and better partners.

From designing AI study tours that land students in Thailand for a week of real learning, to placing K–12 students from Myanmar and China into world-class international schools, to running a global science competition — the common thread is always the same: connecting the right opportunity to the right student.
