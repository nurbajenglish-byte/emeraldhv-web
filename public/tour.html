What Parents Need to Know

Is it safe?

Wichai Wittaya is one of Chiang Mai's safest, most established schools. Local guides + co-instructors with first-aid. Daily check-ins via WhatsApp group. 24/7 emergency contact.

What about meals?

All meals included in the price. Halal-certified, Thai and international options. Dietary accommodations noted at registration. Students eat together (immersion + safety).

Flights & transfers?

Flights from Hanoi included (Thai AirAsia group fares). Airport pickups in Chiang Mai included. Ground transport all week included. You drop them at the airport, we handle the rest.

Will they actually learn?

Arduino + Python coding (hands-on). AI fundamentals + prompt engineering. Pitch practice + presentation skills. Video production (real skills, job-ready).

Can my parent/teacher come?

Yes. One free chaperone per 3 students. Parents pay $50 for the 3-day finals pass (covers awards + reception). Teachers can attend free.

Frequently Asked Questions

What if my child gets sick during the trip? ▼
Partner clinic 10 min away, 24/7 support from instructors. Travel insurance recommended (covers medical). Daily wellness checks. We've never had a serious incident in 3 years.
Can parents visit during the week? ▼
Not recommended (disrupts immersion), but possible for 1 afternoon with prior arrangement. Email tours@emeraldhv.com to discuss.
Can we pay in installments? ▼
Yes. $200 deposit now, $245 in 4 weeks, $250 in 8 weeks. Email tours@emeraldhv.com.
Do you offer school discounts? ▼
Absolutely. 10+ students from same school = $650 Chiang Mai, $850 Bangkok. Email for quote.
Is it appropriate for beginners? ▼
Yes. Curriculum is designed for ages 14–18 with NO prior experience. We start from zero. Advanced students get tiered instruction.
What if we need to cancel? ▼
Full refund if cancelled 30 days before departure. 50% refund 14–30 days. No refund within 2 weeks (we've paid deposits).
