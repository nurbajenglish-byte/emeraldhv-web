Questions? We Answer Them.

Can I compete solo or do I need a team? ▼
Both! Solo and team entrants are judged separately and compete for their own medals. No competition between the two formats—equal prestige, separate categories. Pick whichever fits your situation.
What's the difference between $25 and $30? ▼
$25 is early bird pricing (until Sept 1). After Sept 1, pricing goes to $30 per student. That's it—no hidden fees, no surprises. You see the price, you pay the price.
Where are finals and what's the format? ▼
Finals week is December 4–6 at Cyberport, Hong Kong. You present your research live (8–12 minutes depending on format), answer judge questions (5 minutes), and find out results same day. Awards ceremony Dec 6 evening.
What if I don't make finals? ▼
All qualifying participants get detailed feedback from judges. You'll know exactly what worked and what didn't. Many students return Year 2 significantly stronger with that feedback.
Can my team be from different schools? ▼
Preferred: same school. Allowed: international teams if you can coordinate time zones for online qualifying and live finals. Best case: you're in the same location.
Do you offer group or school discounts? ▼
Yes. 10+ students from the same school = $20/student early bird (save $5 per person). Email competitions@emeraldhv.com for school bulk pricing quotes.
