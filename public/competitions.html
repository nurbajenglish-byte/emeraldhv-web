Questions? Answers.

Can I compete solo or do I need a team? ▼
Both! Solo and team entrants are judged separately and compete for their own medals. No competition between the two—equal prestige.
What's the difference between early bird and standard? ▼
Early bird is $25/student (ends Sept 30). Standard is $30/student. That's it—no hidden fees.
Where are finals and when? ▼
December 4–6, 2026 at Cyberport, Hong Kong (or Bangkok as backup). All competitions happen the same week.
What if I don't make finals? ▼
All qualifiers get detailed feedback from judges on their submission. Many return Year 2 stronger.
Do you offer school discounts? ▼
Yes. 10+ students from same school = $20/student. Email competitions@emeraldhv.com for quote.
Can I enter multiple competitions? ▼
Yes. You can enter A-ISPC + A-IBFC + Emerald Prize in the same finals week. All happen simultaneously but don't conflict.

Ready to Compete?
